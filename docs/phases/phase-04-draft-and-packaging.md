# Phase 04 - Draft And Packaging

## Functional Feature Outcome

The workflow generates channel-aware drafts and packaging together, with reuse and publish-speed decisions built into the same lane.

## Why This Phase Exists

Packaging often changes the hook, structure, and even the angle. Treating it as a late stage slows iteration and hides channel mismatch until too late. This phase combines drafting and packaging so teams can ship faster and intentionally decide whether to publish now, reuse, or hold for more proof.

## Scope

- co-design script development and packaging instead of treating packaging as an output-only step
- generate reuse and derivative opportunities from approved arguments
- define a publish-now versus hold-for-proof path before visual planning begins

## Dependencies

- Phase 03 must produce an approved thesis, evidence state, and uncertainty status
- current scripting and packaging outputs should stay reviewable in existing operator surfaces

## Exit Criteria

- every approved draft includes packaging aligned to its channel and content type
- reuse opportunities are captured before visual planning starts
- operators can decide to publish now, hold, or recycle without forcing the asset through later stages
